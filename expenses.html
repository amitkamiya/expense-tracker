Types of Expenses

Common Types of Expenses

  • Flexible Expenses
  • Fixed Expenses
  • Variable Expenses
  • Essential Expenses
Type of Expense Explanation
Flexible Expenses Discretionary purchases that can be altered or eliminated, e.g., entertainment, hobbies.
Fixed Expenses Constant costs paid at regular intervals, e.g., rent, subscriptions.
Variable Expenses Costs that change, e.g., groceries, utility bills.
Essential Expenses Necessary costs, e.g., housing, taxes, health insurance.
